Other Resources

Direct links to grant portals, funding programs, and technical assistance resources for NYS municipalities.

Grant Portals
NYS SFS Grant Search ↗
Search the Statewide Financial System directly for all currently advertised state grant opportunities.
Grants.gov ↗
The federal government's central database of grant opportunities across all agencies. Required for most federal grant applications.
SAM.gov ↗
System for Award Management — required registration for federal grants and contracts. Also lists federal funding opportunities.
NYS CFA Portal ↗
New York State's Consolidated Funding Application — one application for grants across 8 state agencies. Opens annually each spring.
FEMA Grants Portal ↗
All FEMA hazard mitigation, BRIC, EMPG, and public assistance grants in one place. Municipalities apply directly for most programs.
NYS Environmental Bond Act Funding Finder ↗
Search $4.2 billion in Bond Act grants for climate, water, land conservation, and environmental justice projects — filterable by applicant type and project phase.
NYS Assembly Grants Action News ↗
Monthly newsletter from the NYS Assembly highlighting new and upcoming grant opportunities for municipalities, nonprofits, and community organizations across New York State.
NYS Contract Reporter ↗
Official source for NYS government procurement and grant opportunities, including advertised-only programs not listed in SFS.

NYCOM Municipal Grants Dashboard

How to Use This Dashboard

A guide to finding and exploring grant opportunities for New York State municipalities.

🗂

Navigating the Dashboard

The dashboard has four tabs accessible from the top navigation bar:

📍 State Grants

Grant opportunities from New York State agencies and the NYS Statewide Financial System (SFS), refreshed nightly. Also includes the CFA banner with deadline information for the Consolidated Funding Application.

🇺🇸 Federal Grants

Live results from Grants.gov filtered to opportunities eligible for city and township governments. Updated each time you visit the tab.

🔗 Other Resources

A curated directory of grant portals, federal and state agency programs, regional funding sources, and technical assistance resources for NYS municipalities. Use this tab to find programs not covered by the State or Federal tabs, explore agency-specific grant pages, or find technical assistance for the application process.

📖 How to Use

This page — a guide to navigating and getting the most out of the dashboard.

⚙️

Using the Filters

📍 State Grants filters
  • Use the Agency dropdown to narrow results to a specific funding agency — for example, NYS Department of Environmental Conservation or NYS Homes & Community Renewal.
  • Use the Status checkboxes to show open opportunities, closed ones, or both.
  • Use Sort to order results by deadline or posting date.
  • Press Apply filters to update the results.
🇺🇸 Federal Grants filters
  • Use the Category dropdown to filter by funding topic (e.g. Environment, Transportation, Housing).
  • Use the Status checkboxes to include posted, forecasted, or closed opportunities.
  • Use the search bar at the top of the page to search by keyword.
  • Press Apply filters to fetch updated results from Grants.gov.
🔑

NYS SFS Grants — ID Copy Button

Some state grant cards show an ID · copy button at the bottom. These grants come from the NYS SFS portal. Where available, the View ↗ button will link directly to the opportunity posting. For grants that don't have a direct link, copy the ID and paste it into the SFS search portal to find the full listing.

1

Click the copy button on the grant card to copy the grant ID to your clipboard.

2

Click View ↗ to open the NYS SFS grant search portal.

3

Paste the ID into the search field on the SFS portal to find the full listing.

📡

Data Sources & Update Frequency

📍 State Grants

State grant data is collected nightly from the NYS SFS portal and directly from agency websites including EFC, NYS Parks, HCR, DEC, and DASNY. Results are typically refreshed by early morning each day. If a source website is temporarily unavailable, the dashboard will display data from the most recent successful update.

🇺🇸 Federal Grants

Federal grant data is pulled live from Grants.gov each time you visit the Federal Grants tab. Results reflect what is currently listed on Grants.gov filtered to city and township eligibility. An internet connection is required to load federal grants.

⚠️
A note on accuracy

This dashboard is intended as a discovery tool to help municipalities identify potential grant opportunities — it is not a comprehensive listing of every available grant. Deadlines, eligibility requirements, and program details may change after data is collected. Always verify information directly on the relevant agency or program website before applying. When in doubt, contact the funding agency directly.
